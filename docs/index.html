Getting Started

Start by inviting HyRoles to your discord server.

Setup#

Once HyRoles is in your Discord server, relocate the HyRoles Role to the top of the role list.

note

Make sure that HyRoles has the ADMINISTRATOR permission. This allows the bot to funciton properly.

Linking Discord with Hypixel Account#

First, log in to the Hypixel network. Then, right click your head. Navigate to Social Media. Left click Discord, and type your Discord tag in chat(ie ChristmasBear#0001). Once completed, your Discord account should be linked successfully.

Setting Gamemode#

HyRoles' default mode is bedwars. However, other modes(skywars, bridge) are available. Use the command -set mode {mode} to set the gamemode that HyRoles will check stats for.
